# Anonymization guide (quick)

## Remove / generalize
- Names, IDs, phone numbers, addresses, exact dates (use month/season if needed)
- Institution names if under NDA (use: "University animal facility (Tehran Province)")
- Raw qPCR Ct values if linked to identifiers (use aggregated / dummy example)

## Keep (what matters to evaluators)
- Your role and process
- Tools and methods
- Deliverables + measurable impact
- QC, ethics, documentation discipline
